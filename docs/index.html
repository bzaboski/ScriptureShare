Scripture Share — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Short version: Scripture Share collects no personal data, requires no account, and works entirely offline. We don't know who you are, and we don't want to.

Scripture Share ("the App") is developed and published by Z-Team Apps ("we", "us", or "our"). This policy explains how the App handles your information.

No Data Collection

Scripture Share does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-in is required.
  • No name, email address, or contact information is collected.
  • No device identifiers are collected or transmitted.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are included in the App.
  • No advertising SDKs are included in the App.
  • No data is sent to any server — ever.

Local Storage Only

The App stores a small amount of data locally on your device:

  • Recently shared verses — saved so you can quickly re-share verses you've used before. Stored only on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • Translation preference — your selected Bible translation (currently KJV). Stored in your device's local settings (UserDefaults).

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to any Z-Team Apps server. It may be included in your iCloud device backup at Apple's discretion, subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Bible Text

The King James Version (KJV) Bible text is bundled directly in the App and has been in the public domain since 1611. No network connection is needed to read or search it.

iMessage Integration

When you share a verse via iMessage, the verse text is inserted into your Messages compose field — the same as if you had typed it. Scripture Share does not read, access, or store the content of your Messages conversations.

No Third-Party Services

Scripture Share contains no third-party SDKs, analytics services, crash reporters, or advertising networks. The App functions entirely on-device.

Children's Privacy

Because Scripture Share collects no data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages. The App complies with COPPA by design — there is simply no data to protect.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the App handles data (for example, when adding ESV or NLT translations that may require network access), we will update this policy and the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review it periodically.
